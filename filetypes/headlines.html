# Headlines File Format Despite their extension, improper formatting of these files results in AIM crashing. New lines are even sensitive to this. Below are line definitions. Title=MyNews | REQUIRED | Unrepeatable Section=News | Not Required | Repeatable | Notes: Supported Types: 'Top News', 'Entertainment', 'Business', 'Sports', 'Extra'. Subsection=? | REQUIRED | Repeatable Headline=??? | REQUIRED | Repeatable URL=???????? | REQUIRED | Repeatable End=MyNews | Not Required
